Proof of You · a story

What you are is not what they see.

A short interactive moment about data, ownership, and the quiet power of reclaiming your digital life. Scroll or tap to begin the story.

You leave traces

Every click, every profile, every form — tiny breadcrumbs. They become maps others read. You think the web is private; often, it isn’t.

A quick step

Move your device, breathe. Notice how little of you is for you alone. This is the moment the story asks: who holds your copy of you?

What happens to your data

Data piles up in systems you never touch. It gets combined, sold, analyzed. The result is a profile assembled without you.

Tiny example

A single tag. A forgotten form. A trace that, when stitched to others, becomes influence — not your voice.

The choice

Imagine a place designed to keep your digital self under your control — where access is granted, not assumed. This is the promise your data should have.

What if the default was privacy? What would you share then?

Small actions

Close accounts you no longer use. Request copies. Learn where your data lives. These actions matter — and tell a story of ownership.

Data Sovereignty

Reclaim the quiet part of you.

When control is returned, trust grows. When trust grows, communities thrive. This is the quiet revolution.
